Unpacking the EtikBuy rating system

We use six categories to determine the EtikBuy rating of products. We developed the rating categories keeping in mind our main aim: helping our users find products which will aid animal conservation efforts and curb animal trafficking and poaching at its roots by discouraging non-environmentally friendly and criminal practices. The EtikBuy rating of each product is based on our extensive research into companies, their policies and conduct as well as materials used during the manufacturing of products.

No direct harm to animals

  • No animals killed during the manufacturing of the product
  • No animals are harmed during production
  • No animal testing

Palm oil & soy free

  • The product doesn’t harm endangered species’ habitats by using palm oil and soy
  • The product doesn’t contain palm oil
  • The product doesn’t contain soy

Carbon neutral

  • The company manufacturing the product offsets their carbon emissions
  • No habitats are harmed by the manufacturing of the product

Crime free supply chain

  • The company has no affiliation with criminal organisations
  • No crime organisations are involved in the supply chain of the product at any stage
  • The company opposes practices that lead to criminal exploitation of people at the bottom of the supply chain

Eco friendly product

  • The product uses recycled materials
  • No water pollution
  • Materials sustainably sourced
  • Minimal and recyclable/recycled packaging

Eco conscious brand

  • Eco-friendly, recycled and recyclable packaging
  • No greenwashing
  • Sustainably managed sources for the raw materials
  • Efforts and policies in place to reduce negative ecological impact
